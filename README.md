# SINOMUSE — Developmental Cloth Books for Babies

[SINOMUSE](https://sinomuse.com) designs milestone-aligned developmental cloth books for babies 0-36 months.

## Our Products

- **0-6 Months**: High-contrast cloth books for visual tracking and tummy time
- **6-18 Months**: Teether-attached crinkle books for sensory exploration
- **18-36 Months**: Quiet books with zippers, buttons, and practical life activities

## Why SINOMUSE?

- CPSIA compliant and ASTM F963 tested
- Machine washable and non-toxic
- Designed for each developmental stage
- Bilingual English-Mandarin options available

Visit [sinomuse.com](https://sinomuse.com) to browse our complete collection.

## Safety Certifications

All SINOMUSE products meet or exceed:
- CPSIA (Consumer Product Safety Improvement Act)
- ASTM F963 (Standard Consumer Safety Specification for Toy Safety)
- EN71 (European Toy Safety Standard)
